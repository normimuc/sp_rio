In São Paulo, the gay scene is concentrated in central neighborhoods such as Republica, Santa Cecilia and Bela Vista. With emphasis on Rua Frei Caneca and R. Bento Freitas.

From Dedalos to Espaco Maduros Bear, Blue Bar and the nearby ABC Bailao to name a few, the scene here is banging.

Medieval Bar is a great one to start for meeting some new friends, but the night always ascends up at the cruising playground of Dedalos, or if your even more raucous, the HotelChilli & Sauna is a few minutes north.
